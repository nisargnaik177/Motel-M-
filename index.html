Common Questions

Quick Answers.

What is included in the room rate?
All room rates include free WiFi and free parking. Rooms come with a flat-screen TV and air conditioning/heating. No resort fees or hidden charges — the rate you see is what you pay, plus applicable WV state tax.
How close are you to downtown Lewisburg?
We're at 187 W Fair St — approximately a 2-minute walk from historic downtown Lewisburg, the Greenbrier Valley Theatre, and local restaurants.
What are check-in and check-out times?
Check-in is at 3:00 PM. Check-out is at 10:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available — call us at (304) 645-6451.
Is parking free?
Yes — free on-site parking is available for all guests, every night. No daily parking fees, no garages. Park right outside your room door.
Can I book by phone or WhatsApp?
Absolutely. Call us at (304) 645-6451 or WhatsApp us at the same number. Booking by phone or WhatsApp also qualifies for our best direct rate.
Do you allow pets?
Please call us at (304) 645-6451 to check current pet availability — we have a pet-friendly option with a $20 pet fee per stay, subject to availability.
What is your cancellation policy?
We offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before check-in. Late cancellations or no-shows may be charged for one night. Read full policies →
